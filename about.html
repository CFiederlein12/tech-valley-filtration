About Our Company

Tech Valley Filtration stands as the Northeast's trusted partner for commercial, industrial, and institutional air filtration solutions. Since our founding in May 2019, we have passionately served our community by offering comprehensive air quality solutions backed by technical expertise and superior customer service.

Our inventory includes filters for various applications, from educational institutions to large-scale commercial ventures such as healthcare facilities, government buildings, manufacturing plants, and HVAC contractors. With over 1,700 SKUs available and partnerships with leading manufacturers, we provide access to virtually all HVAC air filtration product brands.

Our commitment to educating clients about the significance of proper filtration, ASHRAE compliance, and Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) best practices has been unwavering. With expertise in MERV-rated systems, HEPA filtration, and UV-C disinfection technology, Tech Valley Filtration has become synonymous with clean air solutions for diverse filtration needs.

Our Mission

At the core of our operation is a dedication to providing the right filtration solution for every application. We firmly believe in supporting our customers through technical consultation, product education, and responsive service that addresses their specific needs.

Located in the heart of the Tech Valley region, our operation serves as both our headquarters and a hub for technical expertise. Here, our knowledgeable staff is readily available to assist customers, addressing questions and providing guidance on specific filtration applications, MERV ratings, filter selection, and maintenance schedules.

Thank you for choosing Tech Valley Filtration. We wish you a fantastic day and the joy of breathing clean, healthy air.
